This is where things gets interesting. Whether the side panel is showing or hidden, or in whatever width at its showing position, you can simply click the "SWAP" button on the bottom left to change it to a horizontal navigation type of format.

Hover on the button will also display the usage.

This is useful in cases where the user wants to change the format/layout of the website, sometimes the website he/she is in might be better viewed with a horizontal panel, while sometimes it is better to view in a side panel.
