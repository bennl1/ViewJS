First, click on the button on the top left. After the panel shows up, click and drag the grey side bar to expand/shrink your side panel. At any time, you can also click the show/hide button to show or hide the panel.

Hover on the grey side bar will also display the usage.

This is very useful in cases where the user has a smaller screen or just the wide panel blocks his/her view. Then, he/she can use this feature to adjust the width of the panel of his/her desire.
